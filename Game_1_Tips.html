黑杰克游戏规则

Rules of Black-Jack


1. 游戏开始时，玩家和庄家各发两张牌。庄家一张牌隐藏，玩家两张牌公开。

2. 玩家可以选择 “要牌(Hit)” 或 “停牌(Stay)” 。每次 “要牌” 会从牌堆抽出一张牌置于玩家列，点数总和更新。

3. 2 到 10 的牌按其面值计算，J、Q、K 为 10 点，A 可以为 1 点或 11 点，具体取决于当前手牌总点数的需要。

4. 停牌后，比较玩家和庄家的点数。点数较高者获胜；若两者点数相同则平局；若玩家点数超过 21 点则玩家输。


1. At the start of the game, both the player and dealer receive two cards. One of the dealer's cards is hidden, while the player's two cards are face-up.

2. The player can choose to "Hit" or "Stay". Each "Hit" draws a card from the deck and adds it to the player's hand, updating the total value.

3. Cards 2-10 are worth their face value, J, Q, and K are worth 10 points, and A can be worth either 1 or 11 points, depending on what benefits the player's hand.

4. After the player chooses to "Stay", the totals of the player and dealer are compared. The higher total wins; if both have the same total, it's a tie; if the player exceeds 21, they lose.
